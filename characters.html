Meet the protagonists of Vice City

LUCIA

PROTAGONIST

Lucia is the first female protagonist in the Grand Theft Auto series. A skilled criminal with a complex past, she navigates the dangerous underworld of Vice City alongside her partner Jason.

SKILLS Combat • Driving • Strategy
LOCATION Vice City, Leonida

JASON

PROTAGONIST

Jason is Lucia's partner in crime, bringing his own unique skills and perspective to their criminal enterprise. Together, they form a formidable duo in the neon-lit streets of Vice City.

SKILLS Shooting • Planning • Negotiation
LOCATION Vice City, Leonida
